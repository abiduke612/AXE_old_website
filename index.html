About Alpha Chi Sigma

Alpha Chi Sigma is a co-ed professional fraternity specializing in the chemical sciences.

AXΣ seeks to bring together chemistry enthusiasts, as well as those interested in the sciences.

Over 63,400 Members           Over 50 chapters

The Alpha Beta chapter at the University of Michigan was founded in 1916 and continues to uphold the traditions of our founding brothers.

The University of Michigan chapter offers numerous opportunities such as volunteering at local schools with science olympiad, participating in FEMMES and Campus Farms, cooking meals at Ronald McDonald House, promoting the chemical sciences through demonstrations, outreach, and more!
