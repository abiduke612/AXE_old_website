About Us

By fostering a strong brotherhood through social events, active community outreach, and collective chapter goals, our group has become an organization of students who benefit themselves, serve their community, and make lifelong friendships. While we are a professional chemical fraternity, our members come from a diverse background of majors and career goals that is focused primarily in the sciences. If you’re interested in joining a close group full of friendly and driven students that is focused on the sciences while offering numerous opportunities for leadership and service, consider rushing Alpha Chi Sigma. For more information check out our website.

Meet the Rush Chair

Hiii my name is Nolan Lysaght and I’m the rush chair this semester! I’m a sophomore majoring in Chemical Engineering with a potential minor in Biochem. I rushed AXE in Fall 2021 because of the professional opportunities but AXE has given me so much more than that. Ever since becoming a brother, I have gained not only so many academic, community outreach and professional opportunities, but most importantly an amazing and supportive community. AXE has become my home away from home and I genuinely don’t know where I would be today without it and the people in it. I’m super excited to rush the Winter 23 Pledge Class! If you have any questions about the frat or rushing in general reach out to me at nlysaght@umich.edu.
