Alpha Beta Centennial Celebration

The Alpha Beta chapter of Alpha Chi Sigma is incredibly excited to be presenting our Centennial Celebration on the evening of Friday, November 11. The evening will begin with an open house from 4:30-6:00pm at the Alpha Beta house, 1319 Cambridge Rd. A semi-formal banquet will then take place in the ballroom of the Michigan Union from 6:00-10:00 pm, with dinner being served.


The Alpha Beta chapter attempted to invite as many alumni of the chapter as possible. However, if you or any alumni you are in contact with did not receive an invitation, you are encouraged to attend and celebrate with us. Please feel free to direct any and all Alpha Beta alumni to this web page to attend the event. The cost of the banquet will be $35. Payment can be made via the pay pal link labeled "Buy Now", or checks can be made out to “Alpha Chi Sigma AB” and mailed to 1319 Cambridge Rd Ann Arbor MI, 48104


Donations to the chapter are also welcome if you are unable to attend the event, and can be made by clicking the button labeled "Donate".


We also encourage all alumni to share any memories or photos from their time in the Alpha Beta chapter to be used during the event. Memories and photos can be emailed to axs.centennial@gmail.com.


Payment for the event is an acceptable RSVP, and may be provided anytime before Monday, October 24. Attire for the event will be semi formal.


Please feel free to contact axs.centennial@gmail.com with any questions you may have. We look forward to celebrating Alpha Beta’s 100th birthday with you on November 11!
