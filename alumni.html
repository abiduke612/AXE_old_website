Alumnus Of The Month

Dustin Meldrum

November 2015

After graduating from UofM with a chemical engineering degree (BSE`10, MSE`11), I moved to Houston to work as a reservoir engineer for ExxonMobil Production Company where I was building computer simulation models, optimizing daily production, and making up long-term forecasts and economics for offshore West African oil fields. I left my job in petroleum to move home to Michigan to build a small cheesemaking company. If all goes well, I will spend the next year developing fermenty food products as well as helping other local food companies solve their food production problems as part of a larger public market development in the Ferndale area. I look forward to certain failure with the knowledge I did the right thing with my life.

Initated: Fall 2007

About

The Alpha Beta Chapter of Alpha Chi Sigma was established in 1916 at the University of Michigan. The current memembers hope to continue the traditions of the fraternity and stay connected with current and past brothers. For more information about our chapter please check out our website.
